Hydraulic Lift Runby Checker

Top of Lift

Measure from the top final ramp (physical end stop) to the overtravel switch above the car. Distance from top car sill to top landing sill when lift is at rest. Clearance between the top of the ram and car frame when lift is at full top. Must be ≥ (Top Overtravel × ½) + 4".

Bottom of Lift

Measure from bottom ramp (buffer strike plate) to the overtravel switch mounted under the car. Distance from bottom landing sill to car sill when at rest at the bottom floor. Distance from the underside of the car to the buffer top. Maximum allowed buffer compression (based on manufacturer/type). Amount of ram stroke left when car is on the buffer. Must be ≥ ½ bottom runby (2:1 logic). Clearance from the lowest part of the car (apron) to pit floor while car is resting on compressed buffer.
