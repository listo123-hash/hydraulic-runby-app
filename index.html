Hydraulic Lift Runby Checker

Top of Lift

Ram stroke remaining when car rests on compressed buffer. Must be ≥ ½ Bottom Runby (2:1). Distance from top ramp to switch. Used as Overtravel measurement. Distance from top landing sill to lift car sill at the same floor level. Clearance between top of ram and car frame at full extension. Must be ≥ ½ Overtravel + 4".

Bottom of Lift

Distance from bottom ramp to the underside overtravel switch on car. This is the Bottom Overtravel. Distance from top landing sill to lift car sill at the same floor level. Distance from car underside to buffer top. Expected max buffer compression when fully loaded (polyurethane/spring type). Distance from lowest part of car apron to pit floor when on compressed buffer.
